Narmin Kazimova (Azerbaijani: Nərmin Kazımova; born July 28, 1993) is an Azerbaijani Woman Grandmaster chess player.
She won the World Youth Chess Championship (Girls) in 2010.
She is a Woman Grandmaster (2015).
