Muye District (Chinese: 牧野; pinyin: Mùyě) is a district of the city of Xinxiang, Henan province, China.
The c. 1046 BC Battle of Muye ended Shang hegemony over the Wei and Yellow Rivers and established the state of Zhou.
As 2012, this district is divided to 7 subdistricts, 1 town and 1 township.
