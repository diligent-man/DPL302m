The following is a list of National Hockey League (NHL) arenas. This list includes past, present, and future arenas.
FLA Live Arena and Madison Square Garden are the only current arenas whose name is not held by a corporate sponsor; however, the former is only an interim name until a new naming rights contract can be secured. Climate Pledge Arena's name is held by Amazon, who uses its naming rights deal to promote its climate change awareness initiative.
