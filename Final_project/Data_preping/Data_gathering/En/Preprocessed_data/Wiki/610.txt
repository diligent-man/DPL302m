traditionally buryats adhered to belief systems that were based on the deification of nature belief in spirits and the possibility of their magic influence on the surroundings
they were led by shamans who systematized tribal beliefs and cults
from the second half of the th century beliefs and cults in the shamanic form were displaced by buddhism which became widespread in ethnic buryatia
by the end of the th century the majority of buryats were part of the buddhist tradition
a synthesis of buddhism and traditional beliefs that formed a system of ecological traditions has constituted a major attribute of buryat culture
