the british dental association bda is a registered trade union for dentists in the united kingdom
its stated mission is to promote the interests of members advance the science arts and ethics of dentistry and improve the nation s oral health
the majority of the bda s members include high street dentists working in general practice providing both national health service nhs and private care and those working in community and hospital settings universities and the british armed forces
