the green mile is a american fantasy drama film written and directed by frank darabont and based on stephen king s novel of the same name
it stars tom hanks as a death row prison guard during the great depression who witnesses supernatural events following the arrival of an enigmatic convict michael clarke duncan at his facility
david morse bonnie hunt doug hutchison and james cromwell appear in supporting roles
the film premiered on december in the united states to positive reviews from critics who praised darabont s direction and writing emotional weight and performances particularly for hanks and duncan although its length received criticism
it was a commercial success grossing million from its million budget and was nominated for four academy awards best picture best supporting actor for duncan best sound and best screenplay based on material previously produced or published
