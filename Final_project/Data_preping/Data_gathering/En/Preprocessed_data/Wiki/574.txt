The Cerrito Government Spanish Gobierno del Cerrito lit Little Hill Government governed almost all the Uruguayan territory during the Great Siege of Montevideo It was led by Manuel Oribe y Viana
Uruguay was experiencing the Guerra Grande between the two traditional parties Colorado and Blanco Oribe sieged the city during years his headquarters were established at the present neighbourhood of Cerrito de la Victoria Other notable posts were Villa Restauraci n with tribunal and university and Buceo with its harbor
