The Cabinet of douard Adolphe Mortier was announced on November by King Louis Philippe I
It replaced the Cabinet of Hugues Bernard Maret
The ministry was headed by Marshal douard Mortier duc de Tr vise who accepted the position out of loyalty to the king
He was not an experienced parliamentarian and was unable to command respect On February he submitted his resignation
The cabinet was replaced on March by the Cabinet of Victor de Broglie
