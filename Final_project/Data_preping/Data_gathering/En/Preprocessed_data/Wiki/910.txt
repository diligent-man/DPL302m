Sky Atlantic is a British pay television channel owned by Sky Group Limited broadcast in the United Kingdom and Ireland The channel is primarily dedicated to imported programmes from the United States and holds the domestic rights to HBO and until the end of Showtime dramas but also broadcasts many original British produced Sky dramas It is exclusively available on the Sky satellite TV platform including Sky Go and Sky's Now TV platform
Sky Deutschland broadcasts a German language version of the channel in Germany and Austria while Sky Italia broadcasts an Italian language version of the channel in Italy Their programming is also dominated by HBO and Showtime series usually dubbed in the domestic language The Italian channel also premieres Sky Originals produced in the country like The Young Pope and Gomorrah
