the picture of dorian gray hungarian az let kir lya literally the king of life aka the royal life is a hungarian drama film directed by alfr d de sy featuring b la lugosi and norbert dan and based on oscar wilde s novel of the same name
norbert dan starred as dorian gray and lugosi played lord henry wotton
it was theatrically released first at the urania theater in budapest on october
