The Picture of Dorian Gray Hungarian Az let kirlya literally The King of Life aka The Royal Life is a Hungarian drama film directed by Alfrd Desy featuring Bla Lugosi and Norbert Dan and based on Oscar Wilde's novel of the same name
Norbert Dan starred as Dorian Gray and Lugosi played Lord Henry Wotton
It was theatrically released first at the Urania Theater in Budapest on October
