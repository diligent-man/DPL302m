paul luther january march was a german physician medical chemist and alchemist
he was the third son of the german protestant reformer martin luther and was successively physician to john frederick ii duke of saxony joachim ii hector elector of brandenburg augustus elector of saxony and his successor christian i elector of saxony
he taught alchemy to anne of denmark
born at wittenberg the third son of martin luther by his marriage to katharina von bora luther was named after saint paul the apostle and proved such an energetic child that his father said of him he is destined to fight against the turks the boy s education began with the study of greek and latin under philip melanchthon and veit winsheim
in when luther was thirteen his father died leaving the family in straitened circumstances without luther s salaries
at the outbreak of the schmalkaldic war they fled to magdeburg and in to brunswick
in july at the end of the war it was possible to return to wittenberg although in relative poverty
on the advice of melanchthon luther attended the university of wittenberg to study medicine
in september an outbreak of the bubonic plague forced the luther family to leave their home once again
they fled to torgau travelling in a cart which overturned near the city gates seriously injuring luther s mother
she died there on december
while in torgau on february at the age of twenty luther married anna daughter of the translator veit warbeck c
returning to wittenberg luther completed his medical studies and on july gained the degree of doctor of medicine
he turned down an offer to teach at the university of jena because of his objections to the theology of victorinus strigel a leading academic there
in religion luther was an enthusiastic lutheran and zealously defended his father s teachings
luther took up a career as a physician
he became the personal physician of john frederick ii duke of saxony remaining at gotha until the surrender of the city on april to augustus elector of saxony
he was then in practice at the court of joachim ii hector elector of brandenburg until his death on january and was subsequently physician to augustus elector of saxony and his successor christian i at dresden
in he retired and took up residence at leipzig
luther was also distinguished as a chemist and developed several drugs such as unguentum ex nitro magistrum perlarum magistrum collorum and aurum potabile which were produced by the pharmacies of saxony
he was interested in alchemy the ultimate goal of which was the production of gold and has been claimed as the most significant instructor of the amateur alchemist anne of denmark electress of saxony
luther died at leipzig on march
his funeral oration was given by his friend matthias dresser
while still a medical student at wittenberg luther had married anna warbeck
they were married for thirty three years until anna s death at dresden on may
from this marriage there were six children
