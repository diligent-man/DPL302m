The Supreme Assembly of the Republic of Tajikistan Tajik Маҷлиси Олии Ҷумҳурии Тоҷикистон Russian Высшее собрание Маджлиси Оли Республики Таджикистан also known simply as the Majlisi Oli is the parliament of Tajikistan
Assembly of Representatives Majlisi namoyandagon the lower chamber with members elected for a five year term by proportional representation and in single seat constituencies The previous Chairman of the Majlisi namoyandagon was Saydullo Khayrulloyev who was elected on March He was succeeded by Shukurjon Zuhurov on March
National Assembly Majlisi milli the upper chamber with members elected for a five year term by deputies of local majlisi and appointed by the president The current Chairman of the Majlisi milli is Rustam Emomali from April The bicameral legislature was introduced in the September constitution Prior to that Tajikistan had a unicameral legislature called Supreme Assembly from and a unicameral Supreme Soviet before
