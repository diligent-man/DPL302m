Screen Actors Guild Awards also known as SAG Awards are accolades given by the Screen Actors Guild American Federation of Television and Radio Artists SAG AFTRA The award was founded in to recognize outstanding performances in movie and prime time television SAG Awards have been one of the major awards events in the Hollywood film industry since The awards focus on both individual performances as well as on the work of the entire ensemble of a drama series and comedy series and the cast of a motion picture
Nominations for the awards come from two committees one for film and one for television each numbering members of the union randomly selected anew each year with the full membership as of available to vote for the winners It is considered an indicator of success at the Academy Awards in acting categories The awards have been telecast since on TNT and since have been simulcast on TBS
The inaugural SAG Awards aired live on February from Universal Studios' Stage The second SAG awards aired live from the Santa Monica Civic Auditorium while subsequent awards have been held at the Shrine Auditorium On December it was announced that the award show would have its first host ever in its twenty four year history with actress Kristen Bell presiding over the ceremony As of Shakespeare in Love is the only film to receive nominations for all four acting categories and the ensemble award
