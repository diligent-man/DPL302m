ITV News Channel TV is a British television news service broadcast and produced by ITV Channel.
The news service had launched in 1976 as Channel Report but was renamed in January 2013 to bring it into line with the other ITV plc regions.
The news service is produced from the main studios of ITV Channel in St
Helier, Jersey
Reporters and camera crews are also based at Channel's Guernsey studios in St Sampson's
Freelance correspondents, camera crews and video journalists are based on Alderney and Sark.
