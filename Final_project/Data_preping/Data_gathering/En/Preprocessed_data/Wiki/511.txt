gerze is a town and a district of sinop province in the black sea region of turkey
it is km east of sinop and km west of samsun
it was first settled by the ionians and was known as carusa
in the region was subjected to protests and controversy around the proposed construction of a coal power plant a project that was later withdrawn by the government after a negative environmental impact assessment
the e e sultan tomb a pilgrimage place is km from the town
the date of its construction is unknown
the region is noted for its traditional domestic architecture
the regions of de irmenler and i demli and gazhane bay are popular excursion places
