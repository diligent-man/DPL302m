ann knox is a canadian former film actress who won the canadian film award for best actress in for her performance in the only thing you know
despite her award win the film had limited commercial release and knox took no other known acting roles
knox was originally from guelph ontario
at the time of her award win she worked primarily as an art teacher at victoria park collegiate in toronto ontario by she was co owner of a wool and fabric shop with branches in toronto and vancouver british columbia
