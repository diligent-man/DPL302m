The AACTA International Award for Best Lead Actress is an award that is presented by the Australian Academy of Cinema and Television Arts AACTA for a performance by a female actor in a film made outside Australia
It was first handed out by the Academy after its establishment in by the Australian Film Institute AFI
The winners and nominees for were determined by a jury
The award was presented at the inaugural AACTA International Awards in Los Angeles on January
