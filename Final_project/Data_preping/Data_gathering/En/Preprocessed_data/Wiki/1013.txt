jola joola or diola is a dialect continuum spoken in senegal the gambia and guinea bissau
it belongs to the bak branch of the niger congo language family
the name jola is an exonym and may be from the mandinka word joolaa one who pays back
there is no widespread endonym used by all of the jola speakers
the primary branches of jola proper and to some extent central jola are not mutually intelligible
