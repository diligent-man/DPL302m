in broad daylight is a american made for television thriller drama film about the life of ken mcelroy the town bully of skidmore missouri who became known for his unsolved murder
mcelroy was fictionalized as the character len rowan portrayed by brian dennehy
the film is based on harry n
maclean s nonfiction book of the same name
based on the true events in skidmore missouri in the film centers around the town dealing with violent bully len rowan brian dennehy
after a confrontation at a local grocery rowan begins stalking the owner ruth westerman cloris leachman and her husband wes
the harassment culminates with rowan shooting wes and claiming self defense while standing trial
through a series of legal maneuvers rowan prolongs his freedom for over a year all the while continuing to harass anyone in the town who he feels is a threat including police
after he violates the orders of his appeal the town decides to meet and figure out how to deal with him
