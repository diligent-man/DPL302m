Khalil ibn Ishaq al Jundi died c also known as Sidi Khalil was an Egyptian jurisprudent in Maliki Islamic law who taught in Medina and Cairo His Mukhtasar known as the Mukhtasar of Khalil is considered an epitome of shariah law according to the Maliki madhhab and is regarded as the most authoritative legal manual by North and West African Muslims
