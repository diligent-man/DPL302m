national car parks ncp is the united kingdom s largest and longest standing private car park operator with over spaces across more than car parks in towns cities airports and london underground and national rail stations
ncp was founded in by colonel frederick lucas
in october sir ronald hobson together with his business partner sir donald gosling founded central car parks when the pair invested in a bombsite in holborn central london to create a car park
in central car parks took over ncp from anne lucas the widow of colonel lucas
hobson and gosling expanded the company by recognising the under developed state of many post world war ii british cities and towns
the pair began buying vacant sites in city centres converting them into car parks
ncp then began managing sites on behalf of third parties
by the mid s ncp had become one of the most successful private businesses in britain
in after a flotation of the business on the london stock exchange was cancelled at a late stage the company was bought by us based property and travel services provider cendant for million with hobson gosling and their family trusts who owned of the national parking corporation taking million
in ncp launched the uk s first private public partnership setting up a joint venture with manchester city council to establish ncp manchester ltd nml
in cendant sold ncp to cinven
ncp began to develop business contracts in the provision of enforcement services for local authorities vehicle removals and debt recovery where by it had become market leader in its field
ncp was sold to i in july for million
in ncp was acquired by macquarie european infrastructure fund ii
in the outsourced services business was spun off into ncp services
in august macquarie group sold ncp to park and development bank of japan
today ncp provides car parking across the uk
it has car parks at some of the most popular public sites such as heathrow airport manchester arena and birmingham new street railway station
in ncp launched a new website and online strategy enabling customers to pre book their parking at over of their rail city and town centre car parks
