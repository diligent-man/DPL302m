the toledo rockets men s basketball team represented the university of toledo during the ncaa division i men s basketball season
the rockets led by th year head coach tod kowalczyk played their home games at savage arena as members of the mid american conference
they finished the season in mac play to finish as regular season champions
they defeated central michigan in the quarterfinals of the mac tournament before losing in the semifinals to kent state
as a no
seed who failed to win their conference tournament they received an automatic bid to the national invitation tournament where they lost in the first round to dayton
in a season limited due to the ongoing covid pandemic the rockets finished the season in mac play to win mac regular season championship
they defeated ball state in the first round of the mac tournament before losing to eventual tournament champions ohio in the second round
they received an at large bid to the national invitation tournament where they lost to richmond in the first round
