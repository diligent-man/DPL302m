Simone Resta born September is an Italian engineer who is currently the technical director for the Haas F Team He previously worked at Scuderia Ferrari from to and from to as a senior design engineer head of the RD department deputy chief design director and from to as Chief Designer
Resta obtained the bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering at the University of Bologna in and worked for Minardi in the research and development department since
In he moved to Scuderia Ferrari as a senior design engineer In he became the head of the research and development department before being promoted to the deputy chief design director in In he was appointed by Sergio Marchionne as the team's new chief designer
On May Resta left Ferrari to join Alfa Romeo then known as Sauber working as the team's technical director However he returned to Ferrari on August
Resta moved to Haas F Team for the Formula One season as part of a tightening in the technological relationship between Haas and Ferrari
