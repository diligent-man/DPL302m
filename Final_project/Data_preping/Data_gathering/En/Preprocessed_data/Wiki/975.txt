the aiguille du tour metres ft is a mountain in the mont blanc massif located on the border between switzerland and france
the voie normale on the mountain is graded f facile and can be climbed from either the albert premier hut on the french side or the trient hut on the swiss side
the aiguille du pissoir m and the aiguille purtscheller m are secondary summits located respectively north and south of the aiguille du tour
