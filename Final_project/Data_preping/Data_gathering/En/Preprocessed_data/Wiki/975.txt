The Aiguille du Tour metres ft is a mountain in the Mont Blanc massif located on the border between Switzerland and France The voie normale on the mountain is graded F facile and can be climbed from either the Albert Premier Hut on the French side or the Trient Hut on the Swiss side
The Aiguille du Pissoir m and the Aiguille Purtscheller m are secondary summits located respectively north and south of the Aiguille du Tour
