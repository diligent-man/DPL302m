peripheral t cell lymphoma refers to a group of t cell lymphomas that develop away from the thymus or bone marrow
examples include
cutaneous t cell lymphomas
angioimmunoblastic t cell lymphoma
extranodal natural killer t cell lymphoma nasal type
enteropathy type t cell lymphoma
subcutaneous panniculitis like t cell lymphoma
anaplastic large cell lymphoma
peripheral t cell lymphoma not otherwise specifiedin icd cutaneous t cell lymphomas are classified separately
