wcai fm in woods hole massachusetts wnan fm in nantucket and wzai fm in brewster are national public radio member radio stations serving the cape cod and islands area of southeast massachusetts
they broadcast primarily news and information programming and are owned by the wgbh educational foundation in boston
wcai s studios are located at water street in woods hole massachusetts a census designated place in falmouth and its transmission facilities are located in tisbury massachusetts
the station was founded by independent radio producer jay allison and his organization atlantic public media with construction and operation duties assigned to wgbh and first went on the air in atlantic public media has also produced local programming for the station
wnan went on the air on march coverage for wcai and wnan didn t reach all of cape cod and the nearby islands however and in the third signal wzai went on the air
