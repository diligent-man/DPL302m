after accompanying jazzmen in his childhood petit went to the conservatoire de paris where he studied harmony and counterpoint
he did the string arrangements for mink deville s le chat bleu album as well as orchestrating the backing parts to some french pop singles in the mid to late s including those of erick saint laurent and y y girls christine pilzer and monique thubert
