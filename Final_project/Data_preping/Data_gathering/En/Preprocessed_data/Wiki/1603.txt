The 1995 Jacksonville Jaguars season was the franchise's 1st season in the National Football League and the 1st under head coach Tom Coughlin
The Jaguars finished with a 4-12 record in their debut season and not making the playoffs
However, they ended the season on a high note defeating the Cleveland Browns 24-21 on December 24 of that year.
