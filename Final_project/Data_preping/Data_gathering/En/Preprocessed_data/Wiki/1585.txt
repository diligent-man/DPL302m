queensbury is a london underground station in queensbury london
it is on the jubilee line between canons park and kingsbury and in travelcard zone
the station opened on december two years after the neighbouring stations as part of the metropolitan line and with its branch was transferred to the bakerloo line in and then the jubilee line in
the name queensbury did not when it was chosen refer to any pre existing area
it was coined by analogy with the adjacent kingsbury station
most of the locale now known as queensbury is actually to the north west of the tube station in the london borough of harrow just across the borough border from the tube station which is in the london borough of brent
london buses routes and night route n and non tfl routes and serve the station
since and together with canons park station queensbury station is the next tube station for the hive stadium the new football ground of barnet fc that is located metres north along the railway tracks
the station and its local surroundings and characters were cited in the song queensbury station by the berlin based punk jazz band the magoo brothers on their album beyond believable released on the bouncing corporation label in
the song was written by paul bonin and melanie hickford who both grew up and lived in the area
