the arve valley la vall e de l arve is an alpine valley located in the french haute savoie department
the namesake of the valley is the river at the bottom the arve
the valley as a whole makes up the majority of faucigny one of the natural regions of france and one of six that make up the savoie region
the arve valley consists of the upper reaches of the river arve whose source is in the mont blanc massif on the savoyard col du balme side
down stream the river passes through the plain of annemassy before emptying into the rh ne in the swiss canton of geneva
the valley came into being during the last ice age when the alpine glaciers extended beyond geneva
