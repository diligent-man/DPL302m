Doyet (Dolhet in Occitan) is a commune in the Allier department in central France
Its inhabitants are called Doyétois.
Doyet has seven neighboring towns:
Bézenet
Chamblet
Deneuille-les-Mines
Malicorne
Montvicq
Saint-Angel
