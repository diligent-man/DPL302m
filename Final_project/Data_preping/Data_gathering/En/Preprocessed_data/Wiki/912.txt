Ziya Pasha the pseudonym of Abdul Hamid Ziyaeddin Constantinople May Adana was an Ottoman writer translator and administrator He was one of the most important authors during the Tanzimat period of the Ottoman Empire along with İbrahim Şinasi and Namık Kemal
He held several offices in the state From he was a leading member of the reformist secret society known as the Young Ottomans In he went with Namık Kemal to Paris and London where he published a newspaper called Hürriyet Freedom
His return to the Ottoman Empire was followed by tenures as governor of Cyprus Amasya Konya Aleppo and Adana where he died in
