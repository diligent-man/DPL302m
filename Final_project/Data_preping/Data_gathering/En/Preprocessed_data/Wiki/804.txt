The Mus e d Art et d Histoire Museum of Art and History is the largest art museum in Geneva Switzerland
The museum is located in Les Tranch es in the city centre on the site of the former fortification ring It was built by the architect Marc Camoletti between and and financed by a bequest from the banker Charles Galland The building is square with m ft sides surrounding an inner courtyard It has four storeys with roof lanterns on the top floor and a total exhibition space of m square feet
The fa ade is decorated with sculptures by Paul Amlehn an allegory of the arts depicting painting sculpture drawing and architecture is mounted on the triangular gable above the entrance and two more allegories of archaeology and applied art can be seen in the left and right hand corners of the building respectively The upper frieze includes the names of Genevan artists Dassier Baud Bovy Saint Ours Agasse T pffer Liotard Calame Diday Menn Petitot Arlaud and Pradier

In the second half of the th century these various collections were enlarged through donations making new exhibition rooms necessary In the Soci t Auxiliaire du Mus e de Gen ve was founded with the aim of creating a new museum In the city of Geneva held an architecture competition to design a new building The Mus e d Art et d Histoire was finally built from to thanks to the bequest from Charles Galland However parts of the collection soon had to be sent out to new museums due to a lack of space
The Mus e d Art et d Histoire heads the association for all art and historical museums in the city of Geneva the Mus es d'Art et d'Histoire The other museums in this group are the Cabinet des Estampes graphics the Mus e Ariana porcelain the Mus e Rath special exhibitions the Maison Tavel history of Geneva and the Mus e de l'Horlogerie et de l' maillerie timepieces and enamels The group also includes an art restoration studio research laboratories and an art and archaeology library with books
The fine art section has paintings from the Middle Ages to the th century with works by the Italian Dutch French English Genevan and Swiss Schools The best known painting is The Miraculous Draught of Fishes by Konrad Witz contained in Witz's St Peter Altarpiece Other major artists include Rembrandt C zanne Modigliani and the sculptor Rodin The museum also has numerous works by Jean tienne Liotard Ferdinand Hodler F lix Vallotton Edmond Jean de Pury and Jean Baptiste Camille Corot The collection of works by the Genevan Neoclassical painter Jean Pierre Saint Ours d is much the best of any museum
The applied art section has collections of Byzantine art icons weapons from the Middle Ages and Renaissance silverware and tinware musical instruments and textiles The complete interior furnishing and wood panelling from several rooms of the Lower Castle Zizers late th century have been built into the museum
The archaeology section displays findings from European prehistory ancient Egypt with a mummy from the th century BC the Kerma culture of Sudan the Near East ancient Greece and Roman and pre Roman Italy as well as a numismatic cabinet
