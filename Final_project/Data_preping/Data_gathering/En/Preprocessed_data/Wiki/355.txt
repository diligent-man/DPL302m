Estivareilles French pronunciation ​ɛstivaʁɛj Occitan Estivarelhas is a commune in the Allier department in central France It lies about km north of Montluçon on the right hand side of the Cher river There is a Lavoir a public washing basin a lantern of the dead and a church from the nineteenth century
