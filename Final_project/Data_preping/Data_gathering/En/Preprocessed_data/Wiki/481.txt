pluricontinentalism portuguese pluricontinentalismo was a geopolitical concept positing that portugal was a transcontinental country and a unitary nation state consisting of continental portugal and its overseas provinces
with origins as early as the th century pluricontinentalism gained official state sponsorship in the estado novo regime
it was the idea that portugal was not a colonial empire portuguese empire but a singular nation state spread across continents hence the name
as such overseas possessions were a part of portuguese identity
the first time that portugal was a pluricontinental country was during the reign of maria i of portugal with the creation of the united kingdom of portugal brazil and the algarves when the portuguese court was living in brazil and rio de janeiro served as the capital for the country
