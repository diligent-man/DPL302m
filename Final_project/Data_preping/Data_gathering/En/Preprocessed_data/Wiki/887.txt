Götamål is one of the six different dialect areas of the Swedish language mostly heard in Vstergötland Dalsland northern Halland northern Småland and Östergötland
However it is also heard in Bohusln and Vrmland
Examples of Götamål features are vowel reduction vowel shortening in front of endings and loss of—r in suffixes as in hsta' hstar = horses
