Janasadharan (Assamese: জনসাধাৰণ) is an Indian Assamese language daily newspaper
It began publication in 2003
As of 2011 the chief editor of this daily was Dr
Sivanath Barman, a retired physics professor and an Assamese scholar
The headquarters is at Guwahati, Assam
It is published simultaneously from Guwahati and Dibrugarh.
