the lg g pro is an android phablet smartphone produced by lg electronics
serving as a successor to the lg optimus g pro it was unveiled in february during the mobile world congress in barcelona spain
the phone comes in white or black
it has two rgb notification leds one on the front and another on the back in the power button
it also supports the slimport video out standard
the volume and power buttons are at the back for easy accessibility with the index finger
