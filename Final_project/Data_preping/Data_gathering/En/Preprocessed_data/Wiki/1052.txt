you and the night and the music is a popular song composed by arthur schwartz with lyrics by howard dietz
the song was debuted in the broadway show revenge with music
the show originally opened on november ran for performances after which it closed
it then reopened on december and ran for an additional performances
authors caryl brahms and ned sherrin in their book song by song great lyric writers say that the musical yielded two enduring hits and a profit although it was an artistic failure
popular recordings in were by libby holman and by leo reisman vocal by phil dewey
the song has since become an enduring jazz standard with notable recordings by frank sinatra bill evans chet baker stan getz keith jarrett julie london jamie cullum and lennie niehaus
the song was sampled on the caretaker s album selected memories from the haunted ballroom as part of the track you and the night
