baseball reference is a website providing baseball statistics for every player in major league baseball history
the site is often used by major media organizations and baseball broadcasters as a source for statistics
it offers a variety of advanced baseball sabermetrics in addition to traditional baseball counting stats
baseball reference is part of sports reference llc according to an article in street smith s sports business journal the company s sites have more than million unique users per month
