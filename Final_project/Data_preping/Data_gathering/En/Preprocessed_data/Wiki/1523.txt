los lobos pronounced los lo os spanish for the wolves are an american rock band from east los angeles california
their music is influenced by rock and roll tex mex country zydeco folk r b blues brown eyed soul and traditional music such as cumbia boleros and norte os
the band rose to international stardom in when their version of ritchie valens la bamba topped the charts in the u s the uk and several other countries
