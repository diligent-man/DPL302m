Mark Harlan is the athletic director at the University of Utah
Prior to taking the position at Utah, he was the athletic director at the University of South Florida.
Harlan is a graduate of the University of Arizona, where he earned a bachelor's degree in political science and a master's degree in education.
Harlan has held sports administration positions at the University of South Florida, San Jose State, Northern Colorado, UCLA, and the University of Arizona
In March 2014, he began his tenure as athletic director at the University of South Florida
 Harlan left USF to become the athletic director of the University of Utah in June 2018.
Harlan and his wife, Carolyn, have two children, Savannah and Austin.
