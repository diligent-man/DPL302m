the hall of columns is a more than foot long m hallway lined with fluted columns in the south wing extension of the united states capitol in washington d c
it is also the gallery for statues of the national statuary hall collection
the hall of columns emerged as part of the necessitated expansion of the north and south capitol wings in the mid nineteenth century due to the increased numbers of elected senators and representatives also known as congressmen with the continued expansion of the united states westward and admission of more states to the union from their previous status as territories now standing of
