anderson cooper commonly shortened to either ac or is an american television news show on cnn and cnn international hosted by cnn journalist and news anchor anderson cooper
since may has been broadcast live from cnn s set in studio l at cnn s offices in hudson yards in new york city
it is also sometimes broadcast from cnn s studios in washington d c
or from the site of a breaking news event airing monday through friday evenings
the show currently airs weeknights live from pm to pm et with a replay at midnight to am et originally from time warner center in new york city and since late from its new studios at time warner s new base at hudson yards also in new york city
a few editions are also broadcast from cnn s studios in washington d c
was launched on september as a laid back news talk program running for one hour at pm et
during hurricane katrina and its aftermath cnn executives noticed an impressive ratings boost of newsnight due to cooper s on site reporting and growing popularity
the executives decided to cancel newsnight and expand to two hours on november
in august the show was moved up to pm et while maintaining a replay of the show at its original pm et time slot
in june however cnn decided to stop airing regular repeats of the show with the pm et time slot featuring its spin off show ac later which featured panel discussions on recent events led by cooper
after being faced with irregular and inconsistent scheduling sometimes being replaced by cnn documentaries or re runs of ac from earlier in the day it was finally discontinued in february
the show is simulcast live on both cnn and cnn international
cooper often anchors the program from the site of a major news story such as his extensive coverage from new orleans and the gulf coast in the aftermath of hurricane katrina and the bp oil spill as well as port au prince after the haiti earthquake and also from the storm zone in tacloban leyte during the aftermath of super typhoon haiyan
