Nawawi's Forty sc Forty Hadith in Arabic al arba n al nawawiyyah is a compilation of forty hadiths by Imam al Nawawi most of which are from Sahih Muslim and Sahih al Bukhari This collection of hadith has been particularly valued over the centuries because it is a distillation by one of the most eminent and revered authorities in Islamic jurisprudence of the foundations of Islamic sacred law or Shar ah In putting together this collection it was the author s explicit aim that each hadith is a great fundament q ida a ma of the religion described by the religious scholars as being the axis of Islam or the half of Islam or the third of it or the like and to make it a rule that these forty hadith be classified as sound a This work is the most representative of the arba niyy t genre of hadith
