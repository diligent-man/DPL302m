Viśuddhacāritra Sanskrit विशुद्धचारित्र also known as Pure Practice is one of the four great primarily or eternally evolved bodhisattvas mentioned in the th chapter of the Lotus Sutra
He is considered to represent the purity characteristic of buddhahood Nirvana's freedom from all that is impure
