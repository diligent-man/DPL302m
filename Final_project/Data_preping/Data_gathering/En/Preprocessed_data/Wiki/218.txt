wuthering heights is an opera in a prologue and three acts with music and a libretto by carlisle floyd
the work is adapted from emily bront s novel of the same name
the opera premiered at the santa fe opera on july in a production directed by irving guttman
a revised version of the work was performed at the new york city opera in with a cast that notably included phyllis curtin patricia neway and frank porretta
