Move On Up is a song by Curtis Mayfield from his debut album Curtis
Nearly nine minutes long on the album version it was released as a single in the United States Curtom but failed to chart
An edited version of the song spent weeks in the top of the UK Singles Chart in peaking at number and it has become a soul classic over the years
A slowed sample of the music was used for the hip hop song Touch the Sky by Kanye West
In Destination recorded a medley incorporating Keep on Pushing a hit by the Impressions also written by Mayfield
Along with the tracks Up Up Up and Destination's Theme Move On Up hit number one on the disco chart for four weeks
It peaked at number on the soul singles chart
The song was used in Joe Biden's presidential campaign
