roland joff born november is a british director and producer of film and television known for the academy award winning films the killing fields and the mission
he began his career in television his early credits including episodes of coronation street and an adaptation of the stars look down for granada
he gained a reputation for hard hitting political stories with the series bill brand and factual dramas for play for today
joff was educated at two independent schools the lyc e fran ais charles de gaulle in london and carmel college in wallingford oxfordshire which was europe s only jewish boarding school until it closed in
he completed his formal education at the university of manchester
after university joff joined granada television as a trainee director in where he directed episodes of coronation street sam the stars look down crown court bill brand and headmaster
in producer tony garnett was commissioned by the bbc to direct the play the spongers within bbcs play for today series
he informed the bbc drama department that he wanted to hire roland joff as director but was told that joff did not possess bbc clearance and was regarded a security risk see christmas tree list

roland joff s first two feature films the killing fields and the mission each garnered him an academy award nomination for best director
joff worked closely with producer david puttnam on each film
the killing fields detailed the friendship of two men an american journalist for the new york times and his translator a prisoner of the khmer rouge in communist cambodia
it won three academy awards for best supporting actor best cinematography and best film editing and was nominated for four more including best picture and best director
the mission was a story of conflict between jesuit missionaries in south america who were trying to convert the guaran indians and the portuguese and spanish colonisers who wanted to enslave the natives
in an interview with thomas bird joff says of the mission the indians are innocent
the film is about what happens in the world
what that innocence brings out in us

it achieved six academy awards nominations including for best picture best director and ennio morricone s acclaimed best original score and won one for best cinematography
in he produced and partially directed a big budget adaptation of the video game super mario bros

his adaptation of the scarlet letter was a critical and financial disaster and his horror film captivity drew controversy with its advertising billboards widely regarded as exploitative and misogynistic
he received razzie nominations for worst director for the scarlet letter and captivity

a movie about faith and forgiveness there be dragons is a project that joff says has a message he s proud to say on film

i feel that i really want to stand behind what it says to us as human beings
