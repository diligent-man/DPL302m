thelwold contributed to the production of the lindisfarne gospels he took the raw manuscripts that his predecessor eadfrith had prepared and had billfrith bind them so that they could be read easily
thelwold was one of the disciples and assistants of st
cuthbert
he is the last bishop of lindisfarne to have been revered as a saint
he is often mistaken with his near contemporary thelwold of farne
