thelwold of Lindisfarne died also spelled Aethelwald Ethelwold etc was Bishop of Lindisfarne from until
thelwold contributed to the production of the Lindisfarne Gospels he took the raw manuscripts that his predecessor Eadfrith had prepared and had Billfrith bind them so that they could be read easily thelwold was one of the disciples and assistants of St Cuthbert He is the last Bishop of Lindisfarne to have been revered as a saint He is often mistaken with his near contemporary thelwold of Farne
