Dorchester was a provincial electoral district in the Chaudière Appalaches region of the province of Quebec Canada
It was created for the election and an electoral district of that name existed earlier in the Legislative Assembly of the Province of Canada and the Legislative Assembly of Lower Canada Its final election was in It disappeared in the election and its successor electoral districts were Bellechasse Beauce Nord and Beauce Sud
