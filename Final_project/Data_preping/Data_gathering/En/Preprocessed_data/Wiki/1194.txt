Robert Neville or July was an English prelate who served as Bishop of Salisbury and Bishop of Durham He was also a provost of Beverley He was born at Raby Castle His father was Ralph Neville and his mother was Joan Beaufort daughter of John of Gaunt He was thus a highly placed member of the English aristocracy
Neville was nominated Bishop of Salisbury on July and consecrated on October He was then translated to Durham on January
