robert neville or july was an english prelate who served as bishop of salisbury and bishop of durham
he was also a provost of beverley
he was born at raby castle
his father was ralph neville and his mother was joan beaufort daughter of john of gaunt
he was thus a highly placed member of the english aristocracy
neville was nominated bishop of salisbury on july and consecrated on october
he was then translated to durham on january
neville died on july
