The Ministry of Housing and Urban-Rural Development is a ministry of the People's Republic of China which provides housing and regulates the state construction activities in Mainland China
It was formerly known as the Ministry of Construction (Chinese: 建设部; pinyin: Jiànshèbù).
As part of US$586 billion economic stimulus package of November 2008, the government plans to:
Housing: increase the construction of more affordable and low-rent housing and the speeding up of slum demolition, to initiate a pilot program to rebuild rural homes, and a program to encourage nomads to move into permanent housing.
Rural infrastructure: improve roads and power grids in the countryside, and drinking water, including a huge project to divert water from the South to the North of China
Also, poverty relief initiatives will be strengthened.Urban Planning Society of China
