Stuff was first published in Britain in November by Dennis Publishing A bimonthly title it followed the success of magazines such as FHM and Loaded in being pitched toward a young male audience with a focus on consumer goods and electronics
The brand took a more lifestyle orientated direction in before publishing group Haymarket bought the title in January and refocused the magazine to consumer electronics
In May the brand was sold to Kelsey Media
